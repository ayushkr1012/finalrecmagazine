About college

📍 Ramgarh Engineering College (REC), Jharkhand Formerly known as: Techno India Ramgarh


🏫 Overview:
Ramgarh Engineering College (REC) is a government engineering college located in Ramgarh district, Jharkhand, affiliated to Jharkhand University of Technology (JUT), Ranchi.
It operates under the Department of Higher & Technical Education, Government of Jharkhand.

🛠️ Established:
2013 (As Techno India Ramgarh) Taken over by Govt. of Jharkhand in 2018 and renamed as Ramgarh Engineering College.

🎓 Courses Offered (B.Tech):
REC offers undergraduate engineering courses in the following branches:


Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

Electrical Engineering (EE)

Mechanical Engineering (ME)

Civil Engineering (CE)

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)


📚 Academics:
Semester system based on JUT curriculum
Regular classes, labs, and workshops
Emphasis on both theory and practical learning
Internal assessments, mid-semester and end-semester exams

💻 Facilities:
Spacious classrooms and well-equipped laboratories
Computer center with internet access
Library with technical and academic books
Hostel facilities for boys and girls
Sports ground and extracurricular activity clubs


🧑‍🏫 Faculty:
A mix of experienced professors and young faculty
Focus on student mentoring and doubt-solving
Some guest lectures and workshops from industry experts


💡 Student Life:
Technical fests, cultural events, sports competitions, hackathons
NSS and other student clubs for social and leadership activities
Events like Fresher's, Farewell, and Annual Day

🚀 Career & Placements:
Training & Placement Cell facilitates internships and placements
Workshops on resume building, coding, soft skills
Collaborations with some local industries and startups
Alumni network growing gradually

🏞️ Location Advantage:
Located in Ramgarh town, well-connected via road and rail
Peaceful environment ideal for focused studies
