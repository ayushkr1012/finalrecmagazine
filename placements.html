Placements

Ramgarh Engineering College (REC), Jharkhand, has been progressively enhancing its placement records over recent years. The college's dedicated Training & Placement Cell actively collaborates with industry leaders to facilitate student placements. Placement Rate: Approximately 40% to 52% of students have secured placements, varying by department and academic year. Shiksha Salary Packages: Highest Package: Up to ₹21 LPA (notably, some of these were off-campus placements). Average Package: Ranges between ₹4 LPA to ₹5 LPA. Lowest Package: Around ₹3.5 LPA.

Stats of placement

Ramgarh Engineering College provides decent placement opportunities, with around 54% of students placed in 2022. The Training and Placement Cell actively supports students with resume building, aptitude sessions, and interview preparation. Major recruiters include IBM, Wipro, and Accenture, offering packages ranging from ₹2.4 to ₹20 LPA, depending on skills and roles.
