Ramgarh Engineering College

Ramgarh Engineering College (REC), Jharkhand, is a government-run institution offering B.Tech programs in fields like CSE, EE, ME, CE, and ECE. Affiliated with Jharkhand University of Technology, the college focuses on quality technical education, hands-on training, and student development. With modern labs, experienced faculty, and active student participation in sports, coding, and cultural events, REC promotes holistic learning in a peaceful environment in Ramgarh district.
