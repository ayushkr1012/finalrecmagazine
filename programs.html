HACKMANTHAN

HACKMANTHAN is a first ever hackathon hosted in Ramgarh engineering college by IPR club this is a 24 hours hackathon in which 21 team participated in which each team has 4-5 members working on a problem statements given by the IPR coordinators. Participants have to solve the problem statements within 24 hours and create the ppt to show how they have perform the solution.

Sarswati Puja

Saraswati Puja is a Hindu festival dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, the deity of knowledge, wisdom, music, and arts. Celebrated mainly by students and educational institutions, it marks the importance of learning and creativity. Devotees offer prayers, books, and instruments to seek her blessings for academic and intellectual growth. In REC Sarswati puja 2025 celebrated in such a way that puja started at the morning time, bhandara at the afternoon and evening started with sarswati mata arti including dj night with multiple program.
